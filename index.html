Four Main Components of Passage.

Passage allows you to experience so many different things. Let's see how it allows you to do so.

Virtual Reality

The VR headset and virtual world is connected to all the components of Passage.

Haptic Technology

Passage's haptic suit allows you to touch and feel all of the virtual objects as if it were real.

Omnidirectional Treadmill

Along with the VR headset and haptic suit, an omnidirectional treadmill is also included in the Passage experience. It allows you walk at any speed, wherever you want without you walking anywhere in the real world.

Shapeshifting Polymer

The running belt of the omnidirectional treadmill will be made out of a shapeshifting polymer that is connected the VR world. Once you come close to an object in VR, that same object will be made from the running belt. So, you can sit on a chair in VR and sit at that same height in real life.

Our Story.

Passage was made on an economic and an emotional incentive. The Co-Founders Armaan Merchant and Sohail Sayed were sitting at home when Armaan's grandmother from India called him just to say 'hi'. We love talking to her, we just hate that we can't talk to her face-to-face. To solve this, we created Passage.

The first Passage prototype was designed at the end of 2018, when Armaan learned how to code VR worlds and Sohail learnt about haptic technology and sensors in IoT. Using this knowledge and advice from really smart people they were able to build an app that's paired to a haptic suit and virtual world that can simulate being in a completely different environment of your choosing. So instead of Armaan having to talk to his grandmother on the phone, they could interact in virtual reality.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does Passage include in the box?
When you buy Passage, you get all you get three main components. The omnidirectional treadmill, the VR headset and the haptic suit. If you upgraded to the shapeshifting polymer package, the omnidirectional treadmill will come separately as we will have to program the treadmill to use and form the shape that's needed.
Do I need a specific setup? Is it easy to use? Any tech support or life support included?
Your home setup is very easy. All you need to do is plug the omnidirectional treadmill into a power source or battery pack and it will last forever (assuming there are no at fault damages or the product isn't faulty). The VR headset is connected to your phone so, as long as you have bluetooth or a headphone jack on your phone, you'll be fine. If you don't have a headphone jack, no need to worry as you can use the bluetooth feature or attach an adapter. In terms of life care, if you collapse or fall off the machine the omnidirectional treadmill will know and since the headset is connected to your phone, they will alert the paramedics.
What's the battery life on the VR headset and haptic suit?
The battery life for the VR headset is 4 hours if you get the Oculus Go model or 8 hours if you get the Oculus Rift model. The haptic suit can be charged just like a camera, you remove the battery pack and charge that. The haptic suit battery life should last for about 5 hours (depending on how many things you interact with and how much the suit is actually used).
If there is any problems, will there be refunds or exchanges?
Definitely Yes, you can contact us at 1-800 PASSAGE if there is any problems with your order and we will try and get that problem solved for you.
